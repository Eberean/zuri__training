Frequently Asked Questions

Why do companies need a privacy policy?

Most countries have laws that require privacy policies for web applications which gather personal data from users. All businesses must have them.

What should my Privacy Policy say?

Your privacy policy must detail the special procedures your website uses to gather data, manage data, share user data and explains what rights the users have over it. It also contains the various ways to contact you

What are the requirements to get my policies?

To help you produce a reliable and compliant privacy policy for your web application, we will need a few details from you. Such as the name of your company, the company’s website, the company’s email address and phone number and the type of data you collect from your users

Can I download the document created by the Terms and Conditions generator?

Yes. Termbuddy not only helps you generate your agreement but also allows you to download the documents generated in various formats such as HTML, DOCX or Plain Text

What are the Terms and Conditions agreement?

The Terms and Conditions agreement (T&C) describes and specifies your guidelines and expectations for users of your web application. It covers issues like acceptable user conduct, prohibited uses of your service and preservation of your rights, including the ability to deny access to users who disobey your rules.

Where should I place my Terms and Conditions agreement?

Your Terms & Conditions agreement should be easily accessible at any time. You should place the link to your Terms and Conditions in the following places: The footer of the website is a really good place to place your Terms and Conditions agreement. Users will know to look there and it will be made available on every page of the website. It's a good idea to provide your T&C to users at locations like the Sign-up/Create account page, the check-out pages, in-app store listings, and when people first open your App.
